About Us

The leading Sinotruk parts supplier in Ethiopia since 2015

Our Story

Founded in 2015, we have grown to become Ethiopia's premier supplier of genuine Sinotruk spare parts. Our journey began with a simple mission: to provide reliable access to high-quality parts for the growing fleet of Chinese heavy-duty trucks in Ethiopia.

Today, we operate the largest inventory of Sinotruk parts in East Africa, with over 20,000 unique parts available for immediate delivery. Our team of 45 dedicated professionals includes factory-trained technicians, logistics experts, and customer service specialists.

As an authorized Sinotruk partner, we maintain direct supply relationships with manufacturing facilities in China, ensuring that every part we sell is genuine, reliable, and backed by manufacturer warranty.
